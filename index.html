Iced Matcha Latte

  • 1 cup whole milk
  • 1 tablespoon Matcha
  • 1 tablespoon vanilla syrup
  • 1 handful of ice cubs
  • Pour milk into a large glass or mug
  • dd your matcha powder and, using a milk foamer or spoon, stir
  • Add your syrup and stir
  • Add ice cubes and give one final stir.

Carmel Frapuccino

  • 1 cup of ice cubes
  • 1 double espresso shot
  • 1/2 cup of whole milk or dairy alternative
  • 2 tablespoons store-bought caramel sauce
  • Whipped cream/li>
  • Extra carmel sauce
  • In a blender, add your ice cubes and your coffee shot. Add your milk and the caramel sauce. Blend on a high setting following the instructions given in our hints and tips. The more you blend, the smoother the drink will be. We find it takes around 30 seconds
  • Pour the coffee and milk blend into a tall glass
